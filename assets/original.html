      Frequently Asked Questions

      How does the estimation process work?

      It starts with a simple phone call or by filling out our contact form. We'll schedule a time to visit your property, discuss your vision, take measurements, and assess the site. Within a few days, we'll provide a detailed, transparent estimate with a full breakdown of costs. There's no cost or obligation for this initial consultation.

      How long will my project take?

      Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity. A simple sidewalk repair might take a few days, while a full patio and retaining wall project could take several weeks. Once we finalize the project plan, we will provide you with a clear schedule and keep you updated on our progress every step of the way.

      What types of payment do you accept?

      We accept checks, all major credit cards, and bank transfers. We'll discuss a payment schedule that works for you, which typically involves a deposit to secure materials and scheduling, with subsequent payments made at key project milestones.

      Are you licensed and insured?

      Absolutely. We are fully licensed by the NYC Department of Consumer Affairs (LIC #1192706 & LIC #1192710) and carry comprehensive liability insurance. We believe in protecting our clients, our team, and your property, and we're happy to provide proof of our credentials upon request.

      What areas do you service?

      We are based in and proudly serve the entire borough of Brooklyn. Depending on the project scope, we may also take on projects in surrounding areas. The best way to find out is to contact us with your project details and location.
